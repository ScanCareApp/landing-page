ScanCare

Application for skincare enthusiasts to learn about the ingredients in their skincare products and verify product legality through an image scanning feature.

Features

Image Detection

Take a photo or upload an image of your skincare product. Our app instantly identifies the product name and displays its BPOM approval status.

Ingredients List

Access the comprehensive list of ingredients within your skincare product.

Ingredient Functions

Dive deeper! Click on any ingredient to reveal its specific function.
